RECOMMENDATION OF THE MONTH

Welcome back, families! This is a great time to check in and reinforce healthy habits that will set your child up for success.

Back-to-School Routines Made Easy:

  • Set a regular bedtime that allows for 9–11 hours of sleep.
  • Lay out clothes, shoes, and backpacks the night before.
  • Pick a set time and space for homework each afternoon.
  • Use a timer to break tasks into 15–20 minute focus periods.

PRODUCT SAFETY ALERT

FunFitX Toddler Bike Helmets Recalled

The CPSC warns consumers to immediately stop using FunFitX toddler bike helmets. They fail to protect riders in a crash, posing a risk of head injury.

What to do:

  • Stop using the helmet immediately.
  • Cut the straps, and dispose of it.
